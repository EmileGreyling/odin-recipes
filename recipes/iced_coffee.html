Iced Coffee

Description

Iced Coffee is a refreshing and straightforward beverage that's perfect for coffee enthusiasts looking to enjoy their favorite drink chilled. The recipe typically involves brewing a strong pot of coffee and allowing it to cool. Once cooled, the coffee is poured over a glass full of ice cubes. To sweeten the drink, one can add sugar, flavored syrups, or even condensed milk according to taste. For a creamier texture, milk or cream is stirred in, creating a rich and smooth coffee experience. This simple yet delightful drink is ideal for hot days or as an afternoon pick-me-up.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine warm water, instant coffee, and sugar in a sealable jar. Seal and shake until foamy.
  3. Pour into a glass full of ice; add milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.
