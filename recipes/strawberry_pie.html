Strawberry Pie

Description

This Strawberry Pie recipe is a delightful dessert made with fresh strawberries. It starts with a baked pie crust, which serves as the base. Next, half of the strawberries are arranged inside the crust. The remaining strawberries are cooked with sugar to create a thick, sweet strawberry filling.

This cooked mixture is then poured over the fresh berries in the crust. The pie is then chilled until set, typically for at least 3 hours. Just before serving, a dollop of whipped cream is added to each slice. The result is a refreshing and fruity pie that celebrates the sweetness of ripe strawberries.

Ingredients

  • 1 quart fresh strawberries, hulled
  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Arrange 1/2 of strawberries in baked pastry shell. Place remaining strawberries in a medium saucepan.
  3. Add sugar to the strawberries in the saucepan; place over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring frequently.
  4. Whisk cornstarch and water together in a small bowl. Gradually stir cornstarch mixture into boiling strawberry mixture. Reduce heat and simmer mixture until thickened, about 10 minutes, stirring constantly.
  5. Pour cooked strawberry mixture over berries in pastry shell. Refrigerate until chilled and set, at least 3 hours.
  6. Before serving, whip cream in a medium bowl until soft peaks form.
  7. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream. Enjoy!
